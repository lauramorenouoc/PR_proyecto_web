CANVAS & CODE

OUR MISSION

Canvas & Code presents AI not as a replacement for artists but as a creative collaborator.

The festival explores how algorithms and human creativity can work together to create new forms of generative art.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

CREATIVITY HAS NO LIMITS

Technology is just a tool.
Human imagination makes it art.

OUR COMMUNITY

Canvas & Code brings together artists, designers and programmers interested in generative art and artificial intelligence.
Through workshops, events and discussions, the community creates a space to share ideas, learn new tools and explore the creative possibilities of technology together.
